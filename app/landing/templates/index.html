Many features to discover with TravelPlanner

TripPlanner is not just your ordinary planner, but something innovational which aims to meet authentic demands from users like you, whose looking for a new taste of adventure.

Have Your Own Account

Customize your own account to your own liking, add friends, connect with friends, plan your trips and enjoy more acccessible features.

Plan Your Trips

As a registered user, you have the freedom to plan trips and to save those information to your own accounts.

Add Iteneraries to your Trips

For all trips created by registered user, he/she can add iteneraries of his/her own choice.

Share with Friends

As a registered user, you are given the privallage to share your planned trips with your TripPlanner Friends.

View Other Trip Plans

Another privallage, is that not only you can view your own planned trips but you can view freely other peoples' trips.

Discover New Places and Informations

As you go on your planning and looking at other peoples' trips, you will discover informations that will be usefull for your next trips.
